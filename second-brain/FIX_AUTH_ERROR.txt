  Firebase: Error (auth/operation-not-allowed).

WHAT IT MEANS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Authentication methods are not enabled in Firebase Console yet.
  You need to turn them on!


HOW TO FIX (2 MINUTES):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1: Go to Firebase Console
   → https://console.firebase.google.com/
   → Click on your project: second-brain-18b46

STEP 2: Enable Email/Password
   → Click "Authentication" in left sidebar
   → Click "Sign-in method" tab
   → Find "Email/Password" → Click it
   → Toggle "Enable" to ON
   → Click "Save"

STEP 3: Enable Google Sign-In
   → In same "Sign-in method" tab
   → Find "Google" → Click it
   → Toggle "Enable" to ON
   → Enter your email as support email
   → Click "Save"

STEP 4: Test Your App
   → Refresh http://localhost:3000
   → Try creating an account
   → Should work now! ✅
