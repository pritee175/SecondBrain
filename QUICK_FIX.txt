🔧 QUICK FIX FOR DISAPPEARING DATA
================================

Your data disappears after refresh because Firebase stored it incorrectly.

STEPS TO FIX:
1. Open: scripts/fix-firebase-data.html in your browser
2. Make sure you're logged into https://second-brain-jdx4.vercel.app in another tab
3. Click "Fix All Data" button
4. Refresh your app - data should now stay!

That's it! The code fix is already deployed, you just need to clean up the old corrupted data once.

Alternative: Just delete all your current data in the app and re-add it fresh. The new data will be stored correctly.
